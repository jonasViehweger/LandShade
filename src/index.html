The colors are based on 20 years of satellite data. The used satellite data was acquired by the MODIS sensors. These sensors have a very coarse resolution of 500m per pixel.
This means that for urban areas and water areas, the signal can be quite mixed with a lot of the surrounding land cover in the color as well.

Also, snow has not been masked out. Keep that in mind for winter colors.
